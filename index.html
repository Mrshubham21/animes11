Astro Boy is a Japanese manga and anime created by Osamu Tezuka in 1952. He’s a powerful robot boy with human emotions and a kind heart. The series explores themes of science, morality, and humanity. Astro Boy became one of Japan’s most iconic cultural symbols.
AstroBoy
(鉄腕アトム)
Pokémon is a Japanese media franchise created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1996. It centers on trainers capturing and battling creatures called Pokémon. The franchise spans games, anime, films, and merchandise worldwide. It’s one of the most successful and beloved brands in entertainment history.
Pokemon
(ポケモン)
Dragon Ball is a Japanese manga and anime created by Akira Toriyama in 1984. It follows Goku’s adventures as he trains in martial arts and searches for the Dragon Balls. The series features epic battles, powerful transformations, and memorable characters. It became one of the most influential and popular anime franchises worldwide.
